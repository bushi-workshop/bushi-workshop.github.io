BU-SHI Workshop

Broadening the Users – A Cross-Disciplinary Roadmap for Social Humanoid Interaction

IEEE RO-MAN 2026 · Kitakyushu, Japan · August 28, 2026

About

This workshop explores how humanoid social interaction should be designed when user diversity is treated as a foundational design constraint rather than an afterthought.

Organizers

  • Kazuhiro Nakadai — Institute of Science Tokyo
  • Bowen Wu — RIKEN
  • Kotaro Funakoshi — Institute of Science Tokyo
  • Koji Inoue — Kyoto University
  • Nabeela Khan — Institute of Science Tokyo

Topics

  • Social interaction design for humanoid robots
  • Multimodal communication
  • Adaptive and personalized interaction
  • Foundation models and LLMs for embodied agents
  • Accessibility-aware humanoid design
